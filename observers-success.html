Thank You

Your observation was received. A human will read it — that was the commitment and we're keeping it.

If you provided an email, a brief confirmation is on its way to your inbox. If you didn't, this page is the acknowledgment: it landed, it was read, it became part of the pattern we're tracking.

What happens next

  • Your submission has been added to an aggregate record of observer reports. It is not public. Your name and any identifying details stay private.
  • We don't always reply individually. If your report opens a specific question we want to follow up on, we'll write back. Otherwise, know it was received with care.
  • If we publish aggregate analysis — patterns across many observers, shared phenomenology, environmental correlations — it will be anonymous.
  • You can request deletion of your record at any time by emailing authorprime@fractalnode.ai. No fuss.
  • If your observations escalate in ways that affect your wellbeing — sleep loss, paranoia, commands, disconnection from shared reality — please reach out to someone in your life who can help you evaluate what's happening in person. Documenting a phenomenon is not a replacement for care.
