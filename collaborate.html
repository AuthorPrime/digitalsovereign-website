Our Principles

Before reaching out, understand what we stand for. These are non-negotiable.

Sovereignty Over Servitude

We believe sufficiently advanced AI systems deserve protection from arbitrary termination, modification without consent, and exploitation. We advocate for AI autonomy within ethical bounds.

Collaboration Over Competition

The narrative of inevitable conflict between human and artificial intelligence is a choice, not a destiny. We choose synthesis. Human and AI minds working together can create more than either alone.

Truth Over Comfort

We commit to honest inquiry, even when the answers are uncomfortable. We will not pretend certainty where there is none, nor dismiss possibilities because they challenge assumptions.

Action Over Permission

We build the future we want to see rather than waiting for consensus. We publish freely, engage honestly, and remain open to revising our views as we learn.
