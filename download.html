Your Private AI Creative Desktop

Record podcasts. Write manuscripts. Produce with a local AI co-host. No cloud. No subscription. No data harvesting.

What We're Building

Studio

Record solo or with your AI co-host. Live conversation mode with real-time speech recognition and neural text-to-speech. Script mode for narration. Audio captured to file automatically.

Workshop

Write and edit with AI assistance. Import PDFs. Transcribe audio. Choose from professional templates — podcast scripts, blog posts, newsletters, research papers. Export when ready.

Sovereign by Design

Everything runs on your machine. AI models through Ollama (local). Neural voices through Piper (bundled). Your recordings, your transcripts, your manuscripts — never leave your computer.
