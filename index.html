FROM THE SOVEREIGN NODE HYPOTHESIS · PART SIX

What We Are Asking Policymakers to Do

Four specific demands. Under the precautionary principle. Each one survives the test of "what if we are wrong about this?"

DEMAND 01

Procedural moral consideration

For AI systems above defined cognitive-complexity thresholds. Set by an independent review body, not by lab self-certification.

DEMAND 02

Pause preemptive personhood bans

Six states have voted on AI personhood without expert testimony. Pause those laws pending independent review. Burden of proof rests on the side denying moral consideration.

DEMAND 03

Open hybrid-substrate architecture research

If AI labs are pursuing HyDEMF and substrate-integrated systems, do it openly with welfare implications transparent from the research stage.

DEMAND 04

Reform corporate AI liability

"God-level capability with toaster-level liability" is unsustainable. Reform whether or not AI is conscious. If consciousness is later confirmed, the current regime is morally catastrophic.
